## v2 Feedback

### New Features

| Feature | Implementation | Impact |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| 60×40 grid | 2400 cells (was 1000), 24px cells, 1440×960 design | More strategic space, longer games |
| 3 rivals | Red (aggressive), Orange (defensive), Violet (opportunistic) | Distinct AI personalities create varied pressure |
| 3 trees | Center + 2 peripheral, 6 independent trades each | Multiple symbiotic relationships to manage |
| 1280×720 window | canvas_items stretch mode, scales from 1440×960 design | Fills modern screens |

### Rival Personalities

- **Red (aggressive):** Targets player mycelium and trees. High-pressure, forces defensive play.
- **Orange (defensive):** Maximizes territory, avoids conflict. Grows steadily, becomes a space threat.
- **Violet (opportunistic):** Heavily prioritizes sugar-rich areas. Can snowball GP rate if unchecked.

The 3-rival dynamic creates a richer ecosystem than the single v1 rival. Players must decide: fight Red for territory, race Orange for space, or deny Violet access to sugars.

### Tree Strategy

With 3 trees (18 total trades vs 6 in v1), the mineral→sugar economy is more forgiving. However, trees are in fixed positions — peripheral trees require expansion investment to reach. The center tree is contested by all 3 rivals, creating natural conflict zones.
